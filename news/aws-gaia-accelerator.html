RLWRLD Selected for AWS GAIA Accelerator

October 16, 2025

RLWRLD has been selected as 1 of 40 startups globally from more than 2,800 applicants to participate in the 2025 AWS Generative AI Accelerator (GAIA) program.

The AWS GAIA program provides selected startups with AWS credits, technical mentorship, go-to-market support, and access to AWS's global network of customers and partners. This recognition validates RLWRLD's approach to building foundation models for real-world robotics.

What This Means

The AWS GAIA program will accelerate RLWRLD's infrastructure development and deployment capabilities. With AWS's cloud infrastructure and AI/ML tools, we'll be able to:

  • Scale our foundation model training with high-performance compute
  • Deploy robot intelligence to production environments faster
  • Expand our data collection and processing infrastructure
  • Leverage AWS's global network for customer partnerships
