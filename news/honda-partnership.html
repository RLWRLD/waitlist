Honda Partnership Announcement

2025

RLWRLD is proud to announce a long-term collaboration with Honda to bring five-finger dexterity to manufacturing and robotics applications. This partnership brings together RLWRLD's robot foundation models with Honda's deep expertise in automotive manufacturing and robotics.

The Challenge

Modern automotive manufacturing demands precision, flexibility, and scale. Traditional industrial robots excel at repetitive tasks but struggle with the complex, dexterous manipulation that human workers perform every day‚Äîfrom installing connectors to handling irregularly shaped components.

Our Approach

RLWRLD's foundation models enable robots to perform tasks that require five-finger dexterity and real-time adaptation. By deploying our technology in Honda's actual production environments, we're proving that robot intelligence can match human-level manipulation at scale.

This partnership exemplifies RLWRLD's approach: we don't just build technology in labs. We deploy it in real factories, with real manufacturing challenges, learning from real-world data.

What's Next

Over the coming years, RLWRLD and Honda will collaborate on deploying robot foundation models across multiple manufacturing use cases. This partnership will accelerate the development of real-world intelligence and bring us closer to robots that can work alongside humans with true dexterity.
